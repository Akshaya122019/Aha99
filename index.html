Our Story

Where Malaysian
Flavours Come Alive

AHA 99 Malaysian Restaurant brings the vibrant, aromatic soul of Malaysian cooking to the streets of Nagercoil. We believe food is more than sustenance — it is memory, culture, and connection.

Every recipe honours tradition: coconut-fragrant rice, slow-simmered curries, and bold Tom Yam broths that warm you from the inside out. All served fresh, all certified Halal.
